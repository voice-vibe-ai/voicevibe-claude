VoiceVibe AI

Hands-free AI conversations

Active on claude.ai
How to Use
1. Click the mic button on the VoiceVibe panel to start listening
2. Speak your message naturally - text appears at your cursor position. Copy and paste work as usual.
3. Say your trigger word (default: "send it") or press your hotkey (Alt+Enter by default) to submit
4. Listen to Claude's response read aloud (if speaker is enabled)
5. Click the speed button to cycle through playback speeds (1.0x to 2.0x)
Controls
Mic: Toggle voice input on/off
Speaker: Toggle text-to-speech readback
Speed: Cycle through playback speeds
